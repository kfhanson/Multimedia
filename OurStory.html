Backstory

Beach Clean Indonesia was founded by a group of passionate individuals who were deeply concerned about the alarming rate of pollution and waste management issues affecting Indonesia's beaches. Recognizing the importance of preserving these natural wonders, we set out to create a platform where people could come together to make a positive impact. Our founders, all avid beachgoers and environmental enthusiasts, were inspired by the beauty and diversity of Indonesia's coastlines and were determined to take action against the growing problem of pollution. With a shared vision of a cleaner, healthier, and more sustainable future for Indonesia's beaches, we began organizing beach cleaning events and engaging with local communities to raise awareness about the importance of marine conservation.

Goals

  1. Beach Cleaning Events: We organize regular beach cleaning events, often in collaboration with local authorities, schools, and community groups. These events not only help to clean the beaches but also serve as educational opportunities to raise awareness about the importance of marine conservation.
  2. Community Engagement: We work closely with local communities to educate them about the importance of waste management and the role they can play in protecting their beaches. This includes providing resources and support to help them implement sustainable waste management practices.
  3. Educational Campaigns: We design and distribute educational materials, such as posters, brochures, and social media campaigns, to raise awareness about the impact of pollution and the importance of marine conservation. We also partner with schools and universities to integrate environmental education into their curricula.
